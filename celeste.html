Game Description

Help Madeline survive her inner demons on her journey to the top of Celeste Mountain, in this super-tight platformer. Brave hundreds of hand-crafted challenges, uncover devious secrets, and piece together the mystery of the mountain. The controls are simple and accessible - simply jump, air-dash, and climb - but with layers of expressive depth to master, where every death is a lesson. Lightning-fast respawns keep you climbing as you uncover the mysteries of the mountain and brave its many perils. Find the game here!

About Gameplay

Celeste is one of the most difficult and the most frustrating platformer games ever created. The controls are satisfyingly crisp and precise, and the game entirely expects you to utilise it to its fullest. Dying has little to none repercussions - in fact, your death count is celebrated - because you will definitely die thousands of times upon attempting to go through the seven levels in the base game, and you will enjoy every second of it.
