Game Description

Explore a strange world full of colorful friends and foes. Navigate through the vibrant and the mundane in order to uncover a forgotten past. When the time comes, the path you've chosen will determine your fate... and perhaps the fate of others as well. Warning: contains themes of depression, depression, anxiety, and suicide. Find the game here!

About Gameplay

Omori is an incredibly depressing game wrapped in a brightly-colored JRPG package. The namesake chraracter, Omori, is a hikikomori or shut-in due to a traumatic incident. He sleeps away his days and nights, travelling through his imaginary world with the imaginary depictions of his friends. The macabre ending, based on your choices, will be absolutely worth the twenty-something hours you will spend to reach it.
