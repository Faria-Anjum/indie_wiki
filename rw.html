About Gameplay

Rain world is a one of a kind indie metroidvania that can easily claim the title of an action-adventure or platformer game. The ecosystem is entirely powered by AI, and the way the creatures interact with each other and the player will almost make you think them to be alive. Playing the game is frustrating, since you will die repeatedly at the jaws of predators. But the sheer joy of finallly reaching a shelter is incentive enough to keep hitting the replay button even if you perish.
